With its playful, fresh, and dynamic design, TikTok Sans is perfect for making your video captions stand out. You may recognize this font as the default font used in millions of TikTok videos. Bring your creations to life and amplify your voice with TikTok Sans.

  • Supports over 460+ languages for Latin, Greek, and Cyrillic
  • Includes variable font with Weight, Width, Slant, and Optical Size axes
  • Offers tabular numerals, disambiguation stylistic set, character variants, and more
  • Optimized for high-DPI mobile UI typesetting
  • Features manual TrueType hinting

To contribute, see https://github.com/tiktok/TikTokSans
